Our Journey

12 Years of Creating Magic

PCR Events was founded in 2013 by Poorna Chandra Rao with one simple belief: every event should tell a story as unique as the people celebrating it.

What started as a boutique wedding planning studio in Mumbai has grown into a full-service event management company trusted by over 500 families and corporations across India.

Today we are a passionate team of over 50 professionals — designers, coordinators, decorators, and logistics experts — all united by the love of creating unforgettable moments.
